My Life Philosophy

"Five seconds of fear, or a lifetime of regret. I chose the fear." - Coby Cotton

My approach is driven by this simple rule alone. In development, the biggest hurdle is usually the hesitation to try something that might fail. I use that "five seconds" to push through the discomfort of deploying complex code or learning a difficult new framework. I’m at my best in open, honest environments where feedback is direct and accountability is a given. I’d rather take the risk of a new idea than play it safe and miss an opportunity to build something better.
